BORN IN A PUB

Batch after batch, brew after brew, whiskey after whiskey we endured, seeking the perfect sauce to complement the famous wild meats of the West Coast of NZ, that up until that hazy night (in our red eyes), did not exist.

Glasseye Creek goes beaut with beef, pork, venison, chicken, fish, lamb, prawns, mutton, rabbit, duck, fish & chips, spare ribs, sausages, chops, bacon and eggs, burgers, pies, sausage rolls, pizza, fancy sarnies, meatloaf and everything off your barbie. Also great as a marinade for meats or to add some guts to a casserole sauce or gravy!

So, in other words, pretty much great with most meats and kiwi tucker but NOT WHITEBAIT! Some things are best left alone!

BORN IN

LITTLE WANGANUI

This area of the West Coast is an amazing part of our ‘god’s own’ as Don soon discovered. It is a place where wrist watches make good fishing sinkers, cell phones are useful as clay bird replacements, the ‘boardroom’ is on the beach with a bonfire and beers and a ‘conference call’ means having a yarn over a pool table with more than two people. Embraced with scenery that is pretty much the way it was when Maui was learning to fish, it soon hits one as a mere mortal, that we humans can make life unnecessarily complicated, and we should be savoring the simple things in life

Family, friends, footy, fresh meat and fishing seem to be high on the list over here!
